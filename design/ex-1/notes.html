Understanding Different Font Types

Fonts are typically divided into several categories based on their characteristics. The three main types are serif, sans-serif, and monospace, but there are others as well. Let's explore each of these in detail: 1. Serif Fonts Definition: Serif fonts have small lines or decorations at the end of their characters. These little "feet" or "tails" are known as serifs.
