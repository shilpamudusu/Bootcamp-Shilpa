Three-Color Scheme Example

Card Title

This card follows a three-color scheme where the primary elements use blue (#007BFF), the complementary color is orange (#FF6A13), and the accent color is green (#28A745). The accent color is applied to links and buttons for emphasis.

Hover over the card to see the color change! Also, click the button below to see the accent color in action.

Summary

This page demonstrates a three-color scheme using blue, orange, and green. The blue is used as the base color, orange as the complementary color, and green as the accent color. The green is applied to interactive elements such as links and buttons, while the orange is used for headers to provide a bold contrast against the blue background.
