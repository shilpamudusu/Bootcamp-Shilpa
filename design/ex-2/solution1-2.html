Complementary Color Scheme Example

Card Title

This card follows a complementary color scheme where the primary elements use blue (#007BFF) and the accent elements use its complementary orange (#FF6A13). The hover effect swaps the background and border colors for a dynamic, interactive experience.

Hover over the card to see the change in color and shadow effect!

Summary

This is an example page using a complementary color scheme. The background is light, and the primary color (blue) is contrasted with its complementary color (orange) for high visibility and readability. The card has a strong orange border and text, making it stand out against the blue background.

Explore the benefits of complementary colors in design, especially when you want elements to stand out clearly. Try hovering over the card to see the transition in colors and shadows.
